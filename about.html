About

My name is Gaurav Kale, and I'm a Silicon Valley dropout

This website both showcases some WebXR projects of mine, and shows you how to make your own as well

Remember the internet in the late 90s? It was sooooo different than it is now. That's because of all the amazing individuals that came up with ingenious ways to serve content on a 2 dimensional screen. I want this website to be place for people to experience what's now possible on the web, and to inspire people to experiment for themselves and become one of the ingenious creatives of our time when it comes to serving content in 3D
